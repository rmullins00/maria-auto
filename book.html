Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Maria Auto & Traffic School ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when you use our site or submit an inquiry through our contact form.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a booking inquiry, we may collect your name, phone number, service interest, preferred language, and any notes you provide. We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, or government ID numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry, contact you to schedule your session, and provide the services you requested. We never sell or share your information for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store Your Information

Form submissions are processed securely through Formspree, a third-party form service. Review Formspree's practices at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. We delete submissions periodically once resolved.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This site uses no cookies, analytics, or advertising technology. No browsing data is tracked.

5. Your Rights

You may request to know, correct, or delete the information we hold about you, or opt out of future communications, by contacting us directly.
