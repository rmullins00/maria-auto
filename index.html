What We Offer

Everything You Need
to Get Licensed

From your very first step — the written knowledge test — all the way through your practical road exam, our team is here to guide you with patience, care, and expertise. We serve students in English and Spanish.

📝

Written Knowledge Test

No appointment needed. We screen an educational video to prepare you, then you take the computerized test that gives you live feedback as you go — stress-free from start to finish.

🚗

Practical Driving Exam

Take your behind-the-wheel road test with our experienced, calm instructors. Maria and her team explain every step clearly and precisely — giving you the confidence to pass.

🎓

Driving Lessons

Scared of the wheel? We specialize in building confidence from zero. Our instructors have guided students through every fear, from highway merging to parallel parking.

🍺

Alcohol & Drug Course

State-required traffic law and substance awareness courses completed efficiently and professionally at our school — all in one convenient location.

📋

Theory Exam Prep

Our staff breaks down traffic laws and exam questions in a way that truly sticks. Students leave understanding — not just memorizing — the rules of the road.

🌐

Bilingual Service

We proudly serve the diverse Dade County community in both English and Spanish, making the licensing process accessible and welcoming to everyone.
