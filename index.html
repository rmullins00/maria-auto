What We Offer

Everything You Need
to Get Licensed

From your very first step — the written knowledge test — all the way through your practical road exam, our team is here to guide you with patience, care, and expertise. We serve students in English and Spanish.

📝

Written Knowledge Test

No appointment needed. We screen an educational video to prepare you, then you take the computerized test that gives you live feedback as you go — stress-free from start to finish.

🚗

Practical Driving Exam

Take your behind-the-wheel road test with our experienced, calm instructors. Maria and her team explain every step clearly and precisely — giving you the confidence to pass.

🎓

Driving Lessons

Scared of the wheel? We specialize in building confidence from zero. Our instructors have guided students through every fear, from highway merging to parallel parking.

🍺

Alcohol & Drug Course

State-required traffic law and substance awareness courses completed efficiently and professionally at our school — all in one convenient location.

📋

Theory Exam Prep

Our staff breaks down traffic laws and exam questions in a way that truly sticks. Students leave understanding — not just memorizing — the rules of the road.

🌐

Bilingual Service

We proudly serve the diverse Dade County community in both English and Spanish, making the licensing process accessible and welcoming to everyone.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Maria Auto & Traffic School ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when you use our site or submit an inquiry through our contact form.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a booking inquiry, we may collect:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Service interest (written test, road exam, driving lessons, etc.)
  • Preferred language (English or Spanish)
  • Any notes or questions you voluntarily provide

We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, or government-issued identification numbers through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, contact you to schedule your session, and provide the driving education services you requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store Your Information

Form submissions are processed and stored securely through Netlify Forms. You can review Netlify's privacy practices at netlify.com/privacy. We access submitted information only through our password-protected dashboard and delete submissions periodically once inquiries are resolved.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tracking, or advertising technology. No tracking data is collected about your browsing behavior on our site.

5. Your Rights (Florida Residents)

You have the right to know what personal information we hold about you, to request its correction or deletion, and to opt out of future communications. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
